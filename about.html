OUR STORY

TROPICAL TOWER is a company that has been founded close to a decade ago. It was founded with the passion to uplift spaces into extraordinary experiences.

What started as a small idea has grown into a dedicated event management and décor service focused on elegance and creativity. We believe every event tells a story, and our goal is to bring that story to life through carefully designed décor and attention to detail.

Mission

To deliver high-quality event management and décor services that exceed client expectations through creativity, professionalism, and attention to detail.We strive to create a memorable experiences through innovative designs and exceptional service.

Vision

To become a leading event and decor company known for exellence,creativity and reliability,tranforming events into unique and unforgettable experiences across Botswana and beyond
